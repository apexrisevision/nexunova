Our Story

Built from
Regional Roots

Nexunova was founded in 2013 with a singular focus: build enterprise software that fits how businesses in South Asia and the Gulf actually operate — not generic Western platforms awkwardly adapted for the region.

Over twelve years we have grown from a small development team in Karachi into a Dubai-headquartered enterprise software company serving 500+ clients across Pakistan, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Malaysia, and beyond.

Our ERP systems manage over PKR 2 billion in installment portfolios, track 40+ housing societies, and power real estate groups, textile mills, and logistics companies that are household names in their industries.
